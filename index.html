Control your computer with your phone?

Today I'll be talking about this app called: Remote Mouse. It allows your mobile device or tablet to control your computer, like a mouse, keyboard, or trackpad. Let me show you how it works. You need to download the app on your phone and get a server running on your computer. It's available for iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac. I like how low-latency it is. It actually depends on whether you're using Bluetooth or WiFi. The WiFi connection seems super fast, while the Bluetooth one seems to struggle a bit, so I recommend using WiFi.
